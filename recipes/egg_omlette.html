Egg Omlette

back to home

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

STEPS

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and black pepper.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chili to the egg mixture.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook until the edges start to set.
  5. Gently lift the edges with a spatula and let the uncooked egg flow underneath.
  6. Once fully cooked, fold the omelette in half and serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves if desired.
