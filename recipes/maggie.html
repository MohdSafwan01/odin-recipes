Maggi Noodles

back to home

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

STEPS

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions, and saut√© till translucent.
  2. Add tomatoes and green chili, cook till soft.
  3. Add water and salt, bring to a boil.
  4. Add Maggi noodles and tastemaker, cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
